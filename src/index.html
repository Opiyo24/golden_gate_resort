About Us

Golden Gate Resort

Golden Gate Resort is a luxurious hotel located in the heart of Yala, Siaya County. We offer a wide range of services and amenities to make your stay with us as comfortable as possible. Our rooms are designed to make you feel at home, with all the modern conveniences you need. We look forward to welcoming you to Golden Gate Resort.

Feature Amenities

Luxury Accommodation

Discover the finest rooms, offering unparalleled comfort with stunning views of the famous Yala topography and lush tropical landscapes. Whether you're here for relaxation or adventure, our accommodations provide the perfect blend of luxury and serenity to suit every visitor's needs.

Dining Experience

Indulge in a culinary journey like no other. Our kitchen offers a selection of exquisite dishes crafted with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, blending international flavors with a touch of local flair with a balance for both vegan and non-vegan options.
Enjoy an unforgettable dining experience with breathtaking views.

Meetings and Conferencing

Host your next meeting in our well-equiped conference facilities. Our meeting room is equipped with modern technology and can accommodate groups of upto 20 attendees. Our professional staff will ensure that your event runs smoothly, from start to finish.
